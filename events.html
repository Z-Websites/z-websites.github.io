CONFERENCE

FrameWORX:The Future of Musculoskeletal Health Innovations in Orthopedic Medicine and Indiana’s Leadership Opportunity

Up to 250 industry leaders, researchers, clinicians, and innovators will gather to explore the future of musculoskeletal health. The event will highlight the scientific breakthroughs, clinical challenges, and economic implications shaping the next chapter of musculoskeletal medicine and Indiana’s unique opportunity to lead it.

From chronic back pain and arthritis to advanced implants and surgical robotics, this discussion will spotlight the innovations redefining care and reaffirm Indiana’s leadership in one of healthcare’s most critical sectors.

Registration is free but required!
